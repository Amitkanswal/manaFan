Core Features 主な機能

Everything you need for the perfect manga experience

📖

Dual Reading Modes

Choose between Webtoon (vertical scroll) for modern manhwa or Page-by-page for classic manga. Auto-scroll for hands-free reading.

📚

Personal Library

Bookmark favorites, track reading progress, and organize your collection. Sync across all your devices seamlessly.

⭐

Rating System

Rate and review manga. See community ratings and make informed choices about what to read next.

🔔

Follow & Notify

Subscribe to your favorite series and get email notifications when new chapters are released.

🔐

Flexible Auth

Magic link (passwordless), email/password, or browse as a guest. Your choice, your convenience.

⌨️

Keyboard Navigation

Power user shortcuts for navigation. Arrow keys, fullscreen toggle, and more for seamless reading.

Smart Personalization パーソナライズ

Powered by Contentstack Personalize Edge SDK

Genre Tracking

Every manga you read is analyzed. Your genre preferences (Action, Romance, Fantasy, etc.) are tracked with weighted scoring.

Audience Segmentation

Users are automatically classified: New Users, Returning Users, Action Readers, Romance Fans, Heavy Readers, and more.

Dynamic Experiences

Welcome banners, hero sections, and recommendations change based on your reading profile.

CMS-Driven Content

All personalized content variants are managed in Contentstack CMS, enabling non-developer content updates.
